Why does a writer go into web development?

Well, I wanted a challenge. And I got it. Learning web development has been one of the most interesting and satisfying things I have done. I didn't grow up using computers often, and didn't play video games until I was introduced to Oregon Trail in the fourth grade.

I've gone from using computers to write books, articles, and essays, to talking to my computer through the terminal, and building programs using some of the most innovative technologies in the industry. I love that there are always things to learn in this field and look forward to years of challenges and breakthroughs.

When I'm not coding, I'm writing on my personal blog about work ethics, cultural observations, web development, and the craft of writing.

I also started a YouTube series called Coders Talk in which I chat with other web developers about their experiences.
