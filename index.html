Backstory

Falon didn't grow up with a passion for computers. Instead, she was rather intimidated by them. Over the course of a couple years, she determined that she wasn't challenging herself enough. She sought to learn something new. So she turned to the one thing that scared her most, computers, and decided to dedicate her time to getting really good with them.

She's grown to love the intricacies and customizability of code. Her aim is to bridge the believed gap between the brain of the creative writer and the web developer.

When she's not coding, she's writing on her blog about work ethics, cultural observations, web development, and writing.
